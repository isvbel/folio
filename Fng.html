Isabel Lee

Melbourne Based Graphic Designer.


FOOTSCRAY AND GENTRIFICATION

This publication’s form as everyday pull-away calendar, reflects the influence and vibrant asian community within Footscray. Broken into 4 parts, the calendar depicts the linear narrative of the current gentrification happening in the suburb; ‘Laneways and Alleys’, ‘Signs’, ‘Urbanism’ and ‘Proposal’. The first 3 parts give an insight to the current stages of Footscray and lead to the denouement, a proposition. It’s a pithy take on the current apartment complexes and the deepening socio-economic bridge between the current residence and the new residence.
