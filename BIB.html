Isabel Lee

Melbourne Based Graphic Designer.


BELIEVING IN BIGFOOT

An illustrative book that explores reflects the mindset of a conspiracy believer going down the ‘rabbit hole’. Using black and white images, and red to emphasise key ideas, the uses found and scanned images to draw back to the idea of the red string and corkboard, stereotypically associated with conspiracies. As the reader progresses, the narrative becomes more incomprehensible, hence the notion of the ‘rabbit hole’.
